Buildings & Facilities

FACILITIES

Baguio City, our campus provides students with modern classrooms equipped with technology, well-equipped laboratories for scientific exploration, a well-stocked library for intellectual growth, and spacious recreational areas for physical development. With a committed faculty and staff, our facilities are designed to inspire and empower students, enabling them to excel academically and thrive in their pursuit of knowledge and scientific discovery.

Library

Collection of books, periodicals, and other educational materials, providing students with access to information, research materials, and a quiet study environment.

Science Laboratory

Specialized facility equipped with scientific instruments and materials where students conduct experiments and engage in hands-on learning to explore various scientific concepts.

Computer Laboratory

Dedicated space equipped with computers and related technology where students can learn and practice computer skills and programming.


Home Economics Laboratory

Practical learning space where students learn skills related to cooking, budgeting, and other aspects of home management.

Mice Facility

Designated area that is equipped and maintained for the care, housing, and study of mice for educational or scientific purposes and research purposes, such as conducting experiments or teaching students about animal behavior and biology.
