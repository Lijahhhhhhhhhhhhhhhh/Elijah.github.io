Our Course

At Baguio City National Science High School, our courses are designed to cultivate a deep passion for scientific inquiry and critical thinking. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide range of scientific disciplines, including physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, and computer science. Through hands-on experiments, interactive discussions, and engaging projects, students develop a strong foundation in scientific principles while honing their analytical and problem-solving skills. Our dedicated faculty members provide individualized attention and guidance, ensuring that students receive the highest quality education and are well-prepared for future scientific endeavors.

Junior High School

Science, Technology, and Engineering Program (STE)

STE program or The Science, Technology and Engineering program is an educational program that focus on promoting a quality secondary education and encouraging student’s interest and skills in the fields of science, technology, and engineering.

Senior High School

Academic Strand

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Management (STEM)

STEM or science, technology, engineering and math is an educational approach in senior high school curriculum refers to any subject that fall under these four disciplines. STEM aims to cultivate critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills in students.

Accountancy, Business, and Mathematics (ABM)

ABM or Accountancy, Business, and Management in senior high school curriculum focuses on teaching students’ information or skills related to accountancy, business, entrepreneurship, and management.

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

HUMMS or Humanities and Social Sciences strand in the senior high school curriculum aims to develop students’ understanding of human behavior, society, culture, and various aspects of the humanities and social sciences.


Technology And Livelihood Education (TLE) Strand

Information and Communications Technology (Computer Programming and Animation)

ICT or Information and Communications Technology strand in senior high school curriculum specializes on teaching students’ knowledge and skill in various areas of information technology and communications. It is designed for students who have an interest in pursuing careers in field related to technology and communications.

Industrial Arts (Automotive Servicing)

Automotive strand in senior high school curriculum provides students’ knowledge and skills related to the automotive industry, specifically in automotive maintenance, repair, and servicing.


Technical Vocational-Livelihood Track (TVL) Strand

Home Economics (Bread and Pastry Production, Cookery, Food and Beverages Services)

HE or Home Economics strand in senior high school curriculum focuses on developing students’ knowledge and skills in various aspects of home economics, such as household management, food preparation, family and community, and entrepreneurship.
