About Us

HISTORY

In response to the growing demand for specialized STEM education in the region, Baguio City National Science High School was established in 2017. It was created as a public secondary school dedicated to providing a rigorous academic program with a focus on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

BCNSHS opened its doors to its first batch of students in 2017, offering a specialized curriculum that aims to develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and scientific inquiry. The school's primary objective is to nurture talented and intellectually curious students, preparing them for future challenges in STEM-related fields.

The school's campus in Irisan, Baguio City was developed and equipped with modern facilities and resources to support a conducive learning environment. These facilities include well-equipped science and computer laboratories, classrooms, libraries, and sports facilities.

Since its inception, BCNSHS has been committed to academic excellence and promoting a culture of innovation and scientific inquiry among its students. The school provides opportunities for students to participate in various research projects, competitions, and extracurricular activities to further enhance their learning and personal growth.

Throughout its relatively short history, BCNSHS has established itself as a reputable institution in the region, attracting talented students and dedicated educators. The school continues to evolve and adapt its curriculum and teaching methodologies to meet the ever-changing demands of the modern STEM landscape.

With its foundation firmly rooted in providing quality STEM education, BCNSHS in Irisan, Baguio City aims to inspire and empower the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators in the region.

Academic Prowess in STEM

Welcome to Baguio City National Science High School (BCNSHS). We are a premier institution located in Irisan, Baguio City, dedicated to providing outstanding education with a strong focus on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

At BCNSHS, we strive to nurture young minds and cultivate a passion for STEM subjects. Our experienced faculty members create a supportive learning environment that promotes intellectual growth and academic excellence.

We provide a safe and inclusive environment, embracing diversity and promoting equality. BCNSHS is where academic excellence, scientific inquiry, and holistic development intersect.

Cutting Edge Education

At BCNSHS, our curriculum is designed to challenge students and develop their skills in science and math. With well-equipped laboratories and hands-on experiences, students gain practical knowledge and engage in scientific exploration. We take great pride in our students' achievements, excelling consistently in regional, national, and international academic competitions. BCNSHS also offers a vibrant extracurricular program, including science fairs, robotics competitions, and various clubs, allowing students to explore their interests, unleash creativity, and develop teamwork and leadership skills.
